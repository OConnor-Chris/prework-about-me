A hipster doofus' tribute to THE CLASH

Bio

The Clash were an English rock band active for one decade from the mid 70s to the mid 80s.
Although a hugely influential punk band, their music employed a wide range of styles including reggae and funk.
M.I.A.'s song "Paper Planes" samples The Clash's "Straigh to Hell"!
